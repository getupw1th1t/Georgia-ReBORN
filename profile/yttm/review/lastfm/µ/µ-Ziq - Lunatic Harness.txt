Lunatic Harness is the fourth album from British intelligent dance music producer µ-Ziq. It was released on 30 June 1997 in the UK on the Planet Mu label, and on 29 July 1997 in the U.S. on the Astralwerks label. It is the follow up to In Pine Effect. The title track features a sample of The Fat Boys' Darren Robinson sped up to resemble drum-n-bass music.
